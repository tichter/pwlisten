Thank you for taking part in this experiment!

During this experiment you will be presented with pairs of 5 second long audio clips. Your task is to first listen to the samples, by clicking on the number. Then decide, which of the two samples sounds more natural DEFINEWHATLIKINGMOREIS to you. Indicate your decision, by checking (clicking) the grey box directly under the number. Then press next to proceed to the next pair.

When you are done with all the samples, a popup will open. Please click on submit in order to submit your work and access your returncode. You will need to enter this code at Amazon mechanical turk, in order to finish the HIT.

Note that we will hand review your work before we accept it. If we reject your work, you:

  • Haven’t submitted your work.
  • Haven’t entered the return code at amazon mechanical turk.
  • Haven’t done the work properly.

Examples of things that might influence your decision:

  • Portions of the original recording missing
  • Artefacts, such as noise
  • Changes to the timbre
  • The presence of other instruments that are absent in the reference, e.g. guitars, drums

Blow you can see a couple of screenshots which indicate the steps you need to do.

  2. Click submit
  3. Listen to the two audio clips
  4. Select the clip, that sounds more natural to you
  5. Press Next
